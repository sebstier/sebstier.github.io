About me

I am a political scientist and Senior Researcher in the Department Computational Social Science at GESIS in Cologne. My research is situated in the fields of political communication, comparative politics, and computational social science.

Recent work has been published in journals such as Political Communication, the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS), the International Journal of Press/Politics and West European Politics. I am leading collaborative projects on digital media and populism funded by the Volkswagen Foundation and on negative campaigning funded by the German Research Foundation (DFG). I am also a member of the editorial board of Political Communication.

Research interests

  • Political communication and public opinion
  • Digital media and their effects
  • Comparative politics
  • Populism
  • Computational social science
  • Automated text analysis
